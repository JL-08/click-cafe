Our Story

Janelle took over his father’s printing business when the latter retired. She is an expert printer and binder and has delivered several outstanding award-winning print documents that rival the beauty and quality of French expert Jean Montruile. Nikki, her sister, is the world-renown restaurateur educated in the The Brasaille Foundation of Switzerland. She created several signature dishes of the Hilton hotels worldwide. Together, Janelle and Nikki, put together a special place where people can have their documents printed, and still relax and enjoy. It is a true marriage of both expertises.
